## Evidence Workflow

The public workflow is:

`Public documents -> AI extraction -> claim/evidence/provenance graph -> classical baseline -> quantum or simulator candidate methods -> comparison -> auditable report`

This is a research-ready proof-of-concept lane. It does not claim approval from public bodies, does not require confidential uploads, and does not claim quantum systems understand documents.

## Public Boundary

The site may discuss behaviour, provenance, auditability, container concepts, and research workflows. It must not expose private ZMath implementation code or private keys.
