## Research Paper Creator Public Workflow

1. collect public papers or article notes
2. extract claims, evidence, methods, datasets, gaps, and contradictions
3. build a claim/evidence/provenance graph
4. produce a structured survey expansion
5. compare classical baseline methods with optional quantum or simulator candidate methods for constrained tasks
6. generate an auditable draft, bibliography notes, and caveats

## Public Boundary

Public docs can explain user-visible workflows and research protocols. They must not publish private prompts, protected internal routing code, raw private user uploads, API keys, or server-only implementation details.
